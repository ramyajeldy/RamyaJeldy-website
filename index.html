About Me

Building AI Systems
Grounded in Business Reality

I started in software — building front-end interfaces, integrating APIs, working with backend services, cloud platforms, and navigating full product lifecycles. I know how systems are architected, how teams deliver, and what it takes to ship something that actually works at scale.

Over time, I moved closer to the business — working as a Business Analyst and Agile practitioner, translating stakeholder needs into structured solutions, aligning sprint delivery with real outcomes, and bridging the gap between what teams build and what businesses actually need. That combination — technical fluency and business clarity — became my edge.

“Then AI happened. And it was fascinating. Not just as a technology, but as a genuine opportunity to build things that matter — faster, smarter, and with real impact.”

Now I build production-oriented AI systems — RAG pipelines, fine-tuned transformer models, ETL workflows, and API-driven services deployed on cloud infrastructure with proper evaluation and monitoring. I bring my previous experience directly into this work: I don’t just prototype, I build with purpose, leveraging the speed of modern AI and ML while staying grounded in reliability and scalability.

Businesses working with me get someone who is focused, structured, and results-driven — someone who understands the problem before touching the code, uses clear strategies to reduce risk, and delivers AI solutions that are practical, aligned, and built to last in a fast-moving world.
