About Me

Building AI Systems
Grounded in Business Reality

I started in software — building front-end interfaces, integrating APIs, working with backend services, cloud platforms, and navigating full product lifecycles. I know how systems are architected, how teams deliver, and what it takes to ship something that actually works at scale.

Over time, I moved closer to the business — working as a Business Analyst and Agile practitioner, translating stakeholder needs into structured solutions, building product roadmaps, Power BI dashboards, and SQL-driven insights that informed real product decisions. That combination — technical fluency and business clarity — became my edge.

“Then AI happened. And it was fascinating — not just as a technology, but as a genuine opportunity to work smarter, build better, and deliver more value, faster.”

Today I operate as an AI Product Analyst — using AI productivity tools like Claude, Cursor, and n8n to automate product workflows, applying RAG pipelines and transformer models to support feature decisions, and building Power BI dashboards that turn data into executive-ready insights. I bring hands-on ML capability to a product-first mindset.

Businesses working with me get someone who is focused, structured, and results-driven — someone who understands the problem before touching the code, uses clear strategies to reduce risk, and delivers AI solutions that are practical, aligned, and built to last in a fast-moving world.
